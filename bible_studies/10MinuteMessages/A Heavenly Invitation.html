A Heavenly Invitation

Mat 11:28  Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest.  Mat 11:29  Take my yoke upon you, and learn of me; for I am meek and lowly in heart: and ye shall find rest unto your souls.  Mat 11:30  For my yoke is easy, and my burden is light. 

Introduction

  • It was October, 2017
    • It was 2am, 2:01, 2:02 Every minute seemed to be an eternity. I had messed up again. The nights decisions replayed in my head like a broken record. No matter how hard I tried I could not get victory over my battle with drugs.
    • I had lost everything I lost my job, my house, my kids, my relationship, everything
    • I remember sleeping in the bushes down the street from the greyhound station just a few blocks away
    • I was tired of being tired
  • As I look around this morning I see many people, from different walks of life
    • I see those that have never really been given a chance
    • I see those that are broken, and have given up trying
    • I see those that are trying but cant seem to get victory over their battles, just like I was
  • No matter where you are at this morning, we all have one thing in common Jesus Christ is inviting us walk with Him, to enjoy His Peace and to Accept His Rest

Notice the invitation

    I love the Bible, no doubt its filled with truths from God from cover to cover, but I cant help but perk up a little when I see that Jesus Christ Himself is taking the time to speak to us here in Matthew 11:28

    • He says your invited to come He is not going to force you to walk with Him, but He wants you to know that you are invited Mat 11:28  Come unto me,
      • Who is invited: all ye that labour and are heavy laden
    • To be heavy laden means your carrying a burden you werent designed to carry
    • He says come, and He will give you rest Do you remember the last time you had a good night sleep. A rest that was not filled with regret, or fear of being taken advantage of by someone close by. God wants you to have rest, one that includes peace and no regrets

    He doesnt want to share your burden, He wants you to share His

    • Sometimes we Go to God to Help us in our situation BUT He wants us to let go of those problems Mat 11:29  Take my yoke upon you,
      • You cant be yoked up with God, and the world at the same time
    • God wants you to study Him When we are learning of Christ, and His ways, all those problems we thought we had seem to sort themselves out. In fact, God says that learning of Him brings rest to our souls? Mat 11:29 ye shall find rest unto your souls. 

    His Yoke Is Easy, His Burden is Light

    • Are you carrying a burden this morning that seems more than you can bear Dont neglect it, or ignore it or drag it with you. Give it To God, Let Christ's Burden be yours, and receive victory this morning
    • God has a victory for you this morning, but have you accepted His invitation. If you dont know a time in your life that you accepted Christ into your heart, dont leave this place unchanged. God wants to give you victory. After I close us out in prayer I invite you to come speak with me, let me show you from the Bible how you can claim His Rest and His Victory in Your life
