How to Approach God

  • 1) You Must Be Saved If we are not careful we get hung up here growing up in America. Especially if you ever spent anytime in a na or aa meeting. You get it in your head that you can choose your higher power, and some of us just say okay I choose Jesus. The problem is that is not what God says salvation looks like Joh 14:6 Jesus saith unto him, I am THE WAY, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me.
  • 2) You Come to Church If youre wondering why you dont feel God's presence in your life. When was the last time you woke up Sunday morning and said, Jesus, you have done so much for me I am going to hear your Word this morning.
    • I dont have a way to church, etc Invitation to Gospel Light Baptist
  • 3) Constantly Seeking God's Forgiveness You know falling down and making mistakes is okay. The Bible says a righteous man falls 7 times and get's back up again. But do you bring it to God, seek His forgiveness for it. 1Jn 1:9 If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.
