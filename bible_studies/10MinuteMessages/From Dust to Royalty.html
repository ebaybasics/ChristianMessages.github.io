From Dust to Royalty

1Sa 2:8  He raiseth up the poor out of the dust, and lifteth up the beggar from the dunghill, to set them among princes, and to make them inherit the throne of glory: for the pillars of the earth are the LORD'S, and he hath set the world upon them. 

Intro

  • Who am I I am grateful for Hope Faith to give us an opportunity to present a devotional.
  • I remember my time at Hope Faith I remember the routine of waking up everyday at the city union for breakfast, getting sent out super early and the next stop was Hope Faith
  • Maybe you can relate I had all but given up completely. I had sat in those seats everyday, tuning out the speaker and tuning out the world just barely making it by till city untion mission would open their doors again
  • But God never Gave up on Me God was pursuing me, just like He is pursuing each and everyone in this room Joh 3:16  For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life. 

God Wants To Change Your Circumstances

  • You dont have to Fix Yourself First God Meets You Where You Are At 1Sa 2:8  He raiseth up the poor out of the dust,
  • Have You Hit Rock Bottom??? You dont have to fix yourself for God but you do have to be done with this way of life 1Sa 2:8  He raiseth up the poor out of the dust, God is waiting for you to realize that the ways of the world will not get you to where you want to go.
  • Wouldnt it be nice if someone came by and lifted you up? If someone came to you and said, heres that car you need, heres that job youve been praying for heres that fill in the blank... Thats what God is promising 1Sa 2:8 "...and lifteth up the beggar from the dunghill" Im not speaking to a prosperity Gospel, im just telling you God takes care of His own
  • He Doesnt Intend To leave You Where He Found You Yes God meets you where youre atl, and if you will accept Him He will change your life but He does not intend for you to stay in the dust or the bottom of the dunghill 1Sa 2:8 "...to set them among princes"
  • Once You Accept Christ You are One of His God says He not only picks u up, and puts you at the table with Royalty, He makes you one of them and to make them inherit the throne of glory: for the pillars of the earth are the LORD'S, and he hath set the world upon them. 

How To Receive Gods Intervention: You have to be Saved

  • 1) You have To Believe God is Who He Says He Is
  • 2) Its Our Sin that Separates us From Him God wants to intervene on your life right now, no matter where your at but you have to turn away from the world and turn to Himto Him Rom 5:8  But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us. 
  • 3) It needs to be more than Head knowledge It must be a belief in your heart. Rom 10:9  That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved.
