Going A Little Further

Eph 2:10  For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus unto good works, which God hath before ordained that we should walk in them. 

    Introduction

    • Going a little further: (If you would have told me...purposed in my heart)Eph 2:10  For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus unto good works, which God hath before ordained that we should walk in them.
    • The question I want to ask you, Where are you in your walk with Christ. (Can you go a little further?)

      How do we go even further? (already stretched thin)

    • Answer God's call on our life (drove into the parking lot)Php 3:14  I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus. 
    • Get more active in serving (I know you serve but Compare your sacrifices to God's) 1Pe 4:10  As every man hath received the gift, even so minister the same one to another, as good stewards of the manifold grace of God. 

      The blessings of going further

    • You are going to see God's footsteps in your path(Go a little further long enough...) (dragging your cross if you have too)Luk 9:23  And he said to them all, If any man will come after me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross daily, and follow me. 
    • You will see lives changed (Imagine the joy Andrew must have felt leading Peter to the Lord) Joh 1:40  One of the two which heard John speak, and followed him, was Andrew, Simon Peter's brother.  Joh 1:41  He first findeth his own brother Simon, and saith unto him, We have found the Messias, which is, being interpreted, the Christ. 

      Where's the need:

    • There's a need in God's ministries (If youre not serving in one of God's ministries here, we need your help) 1Co 12:14  For the body is not one member, but many. 
    • There's a need in your prayers (Don't discount the power of prayer) Jer 33:3  Call unto me, and I will answer thee, and shew thee great and mighty things, which thou knowest not. 

      Closing:

    • Have you gone as far as you are willing to go for God? (will you tell God He put too much on your plate)
    • Is there something in your life God is asking for, (maybe your time, prayers or giving)
    • Will you go a little further for God?
