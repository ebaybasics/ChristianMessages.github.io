Importance of the Truth

  • Truth - How to Survive This truth gives us all we need to survive the battle Psa 18:30  As for God, his way is perfect: the word of the LORD is tried: he is a buckler (*a shield) to all those that trust in him. 
  • Truth is How to Attack If we are to be good soldiers, we need to know how to use our weapon - The Bible is our Sword Heb 4:12  For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword, piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart. 
  • Truth tells us the Purpose of the Battle Why have we been under attack, why does the devil hate us, we exist in the middle of a war and the Bible explains why Eph 6:12  For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places. 
  • Truth tells us the Victors If your saved today, youre on the winning side. One day, every knee will bow to God Isa 45:22-23  Look unto me, and be ye saved, all the ends of the earth: for I am God, and there is none else. I have sworn by myself, the word is gone out of my mouth in righteousness, and shall not return, That unto me every knee shall bow, every tongue shall swear. 

Lies of the World

  • We Can Live as We Choose One of the biggest lies is that we can do as we please, it doesnt matter 1Jn 1:6-7  If we say that we have fellowship with him, and walk in darkness, we lie, and do not the truth: But if we walk in the light, as he is in the light, we have fellowship one with another, and the blood of Jesus Christ his Son cleanseth us from all sin. 
  • There's no difference between right and wrong The world rejects God because they want to live in sin, so they deny the existence of sin 1Jn 1:8  If we say that we have no sin, we deceive ourselves, and the truth is not in us. 
  • Christian Liberties So-called Christians will take the Bible out of context and say they have liberty to sin - Gal 5:13  For, brethren, ye have been called unto liberty; only use not liberty for an occasion to the flesh, but by love serve one another. 

What is the Truth

  • Definition Websters 1828 Conformity to fact or reality; exact accordance with that which is, or has been, or shall be. The truth of history constitutes its whole value. We rely on the truth of the scriptural prophecies
  • His Word is Truth Jesus's prayer right before He was arrested - Asking His father to set apart and make Holy those that Believe on Jesus Joh 17:17 Sanctify them through thy truth: thy word is truth.
  • Truth is Sincerity (Jesus is speaking to the Samaritan woman, The Lord is seeking sincere worshippers) Joh 4:23  But the hour cometh, and now is, when the true (*sincere) worshippers shall worship the Father in spirit and in truth (*with sincerity): for the Father seeketh such to worship him.

Jesus said unto him, If thou canst believe, all things are possible to him that believeth.

  • Be His Disciple (you want to live as a free man, you must follow Him, evidence of this will be that you put others first) Mat 16:24-25 Then said Jesus unto his disciples, If any man will come after me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross, and follow me. For whosoever will save his life shall lose it: and whosoever will lose his life for my sake shall find it.
  • Learn of Jesus Our goal is to be more like Him, and less like us 2Pe 3:18 But grow in grace, and in the knowledge of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ. To him be glory both now and for ever. Amen.

Remaining Free - Our Text John 8:31-32

  • Increase Our Faith The faith that Peter had when he walked on water was not the same faith he had when he first confessed that the Lord was the messiah “ Then said Jesus to those Jews which believed on him”
  • Obedience We must apply God's word to our life, it is not enough that we hear and believe but we must do “…If ye continue (*implying there is an action involved) in my word”
  • Know the Truth To remain free, we must continue to know Him and understand Him by reading the Word daily. “And ye shall know the truth (*You know it by reading it daily),”
  • Live Free Some people our unshackled and the prison gate opens yet they remain right where there at “and the truth shall make you free. (*But you must continue to live free - God will not force you to be free) “
