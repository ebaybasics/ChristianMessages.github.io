Found Treasure
  • Useful References
    • His people as the treasure
      • Exo 19:5  Now therefore, if ye will obey my voice indeed, and keep my covenant, then ye shall be a peculiar treasure unto me above all people: for all the earth is mine: 
      • Deu 4:20  But the LORD hath taken you, and brought you forth out of the iron furnace, even out of Egypt, to be unto him a people of inheritance, as ye are this day. 
    • His truth is the treasure
      • Psa 119:72  The law of thy mouth is better unto me than thousands of gold and silver. 
      • Psa 119:127  Therefore I love thy commandments above gold; yea, above fine gold. 
  • Thoughts on flow:
    • Intro: Why are you here?
    • What is the treasure? (the goal, purpose, truth)
      • The treasure is peace: Mat 11:28  Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest. 
      • The treasure is purpose: Philippians 3:14 I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus.
      • The treasure is privilege: Romans 6:18, "Being then made free from sin, ye became the servants of righteousness."
    • How to find the treasure?
      • Seek Him: Mat 6:33  But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you. 
      • Remember who you are: Rom 5:8  But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us. 
      • Mat 4:17  From that time Jesus began to preach, and to say, Repent: for the kingdom of heaven is at hand. 
      • Surrender to Him Rom 10:9  That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved. 
    • Results of Finding the Treasure
      • Some things are going to change in your life: 2Corinthians 5:17 Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new.
      • You are going to receive the blessings of God: Psa 1:1  Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful. 
    • Outro: God has more for you
