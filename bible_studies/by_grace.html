By Grace

Why are you here? Pure motives lead to life-changing decisions being made Isaiah 55:12a "For ye shall go out with joy, and be led forth with peace:"

Eph 2:8-9 "For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God: Not of works, lest any man should boast."

    Introduction

    • What is Grace?Websters 1828 defines Grace as the free, unmerited love and favor of God.
    • God's plan of Salvation is the same for everyone:
      • Joh 14:6  Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me. 
      • Act 10:35 "But in every nation he that feareth him, and worketh righteousness, is accepted with him."
    • Nobody earns or deserves Salvation: (I am glad for that) Eph 2:8-9b "...it is the gift of God: Not of works, lest any man should boast."

    Why did God sacrifice Himself for us?: -Our Text: "For by grace..."

    • Number 1 He is God and He chose to:Rom 11:36 "For of him, and through him, and to him, are all things: to whom be glory for ever. Amen. "
    • He did it because He loves us:John 3:16 "For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life. "

    The Blessings of Salvation: -Our Text: "...it is the gift of God:..."

    • You will start getting victories over things holding you down:Rom 6:14  "For sin shall not have dominion over you: for ye are not under the law, but under grace."
    • You will know peace in your life:Isaiah 26:3 "Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee: because he trusteth in thee."
    • Your past will no longer haunt you:Psalm 103:12 "As far as the east is from the west, so far hath he removed our transgressions from us.

      The Requirements of Salvation:

    • You must believe that God is who He says He is:Eph 2:8 "For by grace are ye saved through faith;..."
    • Our sin separates us from God, and a price has to be paid: something has to give Rom 6:23 "For the wages of sin is death..."
    • We have a choice, we pay for our debt or accept God's sacrifice/gift for us: (short of money at the register) (being in the courtroom multiple life sentences)Rom 6:23b "...but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord."

    What are you going to do with it?:

    • Will you accept the peace and blessings that can only come through His salvation?John 3:36 "He that believeth on the Son hath everlasting life:..."
    • Will you reject God and His gift?Joh 12:48 "He that rejecteth me, and receiveth not my words, hath one that judgeth him: the word that I have spoken, the same shall judge him in the last day. "
    • You might not get another chance:Jas 4:14 "Whereas ye know not what shall be on the morrow. For what is your life? It is even a vapour, that appeareth for a little time, and then vanisheth away. "

    Will you accept His gift?:

    • If you've never experienced the blessings of God NOW is the time
    • Have you fallen away from God? Dont leave here today without seeking His face. God is quick to forgive
