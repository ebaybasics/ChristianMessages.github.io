A Growing Faith

Heb 11:6  BUT WITHOUT FAITH it is impossible to please him: for he that cometh to God must believe that he is, and that he is a rewarder of them that diligently seek him. 

The Birth of Faith

  • The Fruit of the Spirit is Divine It is not produced by man, but by God through man Unless Christ lives within you, you cannot produce this fruit
  • Faith is Born When We First:
    • Recognize Who God Is Gen 1:1  In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth. 
    • Understand His Intention for Our Lives He desires to have a relationship with you, that He can influence your life and you will be His workmanship Eph 2:10  For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus unto good works, which God hath before ordained that we should walk in them. 
    • Understand The Wall That Separates us from Christ Rom 3:23  For all have SINned, and come short of the glory of God; 
    • Realize There Is No Sin To Big Except Rejecting His Gift Mat 12:31  Wherefore I say unto you, All manner of sin and blasphemy shall be forgiven unto men: but the blasphemy against the Holy Ghost shall not be forgiven unto men.
    • Recognize Our Need For Him and Call Upon Him Sometimes God allows things to happen to us to put us in a position where we are looking for the way out. Rom 10:9  That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved. 

The Growth of Faith

  • A Complicated Life Made Simple Once Christ moves in, our life simplifies into two choices: 1) Walk in the path that Christ has set forth or 2) Choose our own path
    • Mat 7:13  Enter ye in at the strait gate: for wide is the gate, and broad is the way, that leadeth to destruction, and many there be which go in thereat: 
  • The Simple Yet Challenging Decision of Walking In God's Path Walking in His direction does take effort simlar to working out. But when we excercise our faith it grows
    • Php 2:12  Wherefore, my beloved, as ye have always obeyed, not as in my presence only, but now much more in my absence, work out your own salvation with fear and trembling.  Php 2:13  For it is God which worketh in you both to will and to do of his good pleasure. 

Biblical Example - A Fisherman named Peter

    Mat 4:18  And Jesus, walking by the sea of Galilee, saw two brethren, Simon called Peter, and Andrew his brother, casting a net into the sea: for they were fishers.  Mat 4:19  And he saith unto them, Follow me, and I will make you fishers of men.  Mat 4:20  And they straightway left their nets, and followed him. 

    • Jesus Pursued Peter Mat 4:18  And Jesus, walking by the sea of Galilee, saw two brethren, Simon called Peter, and Andrew his brother, casting a net into the sea: for they were fishers.  Mat 4:19  And he saith unto them, Follow me, and I will make you fishers of men. 
      • The World Will Say they Are trying to Find God but God is Pursuing You
    • Peter Had a Life Before Christ Peter was doing something he enjoyed when Christ called him. He was fishing. Likely he was working to improve his life, as fishing was done to support oneself. He didnt let that keep him from following Christ Mat 4:20  And they straightway LEFT THEIR NETS, and followed him. 
      • What is it your holding onto. What is keeping you from making a decision to follow after Christ Is it a place to stay, food, a job etc. God promises that if you will seek him First, He will take care of your needs Mat 6:31  Therefore take no thought, saying, What shall we eat? or, What shall we drink? or, Wherewithal shall we be clothed?  Mat 6:32  (For after all these things do the Gentiles seek:) for your heavenly Father knoweth that ye have need of all these things.  Mat 6:33  But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you. 
    • Peter Was Willing To Follow In order to be in a position for God to use you, and to prove Himself to you you have to submit to Him. Mat 4:20  And they straightway left their nets, and FOLLOWED HIM.

    Peter's Maturing Faith

    • Peter's Faith Didn't Come All At Once Peter's faith had been growing throughout Christ's ministry. At this point He'd seen some things Jesus could do
      • Jesus Healed the Leper Peter seen through faith Jesus healing the leper (contagious diseased man) Mat 8:2 And, behold, there came a leper and worshipped him, saying, Lord, if thou wilt, thou canst make me clean. (Notice The Lepers Faith) Mat 8:3 And Jesus put forth his hand, and touched him, saying, I will; be thou clean. And immediately his leprosy was cleansed.
      • Jesus Healed the Crippled Man Mat 9:2 And, behold, they brought to him a man sick of the palsy, lying on a bed: and Jesus seeing their faith said unto the sick of the palsy; Son, be of good cheer; thy sins be forgiven thee.
      • Jesus Gave The Blind Sight Mat 9:28 And when he was come into the house, the blind men came to him: and Jesus saith unto them, Believe ye that I am able to do this? They said unto him, Yea, Lord. Mat 9:29 Then touched he their eyes, saying, According to your faith be it unto you.
    • God Is Willing To Increase Your Faith His only requirement is that we will choose to follow Him in as big of matters AS WE CAN, and He will prove Himself to you. Luk 17:5  And the apostles said unto the Lord, Increase our faith. 
      • This is What Christ Wants - He Can Handle Your Skepticism but Desires Your Willingness

        Getting Off The Boat

        • Answer God's Call When God is talking to you, make a decision to follow God's call on your life. Mat 14:29 And he said, Come.
        • Take An Action Step Towards God Dont just hear His invitation, Act on it. Draw Nigh to God. I can imagine as Peter was stepping toward God that God was stepping toward Peter Jas 4:8 Draw nigh to God, and he will draw nigh to you.
        • Excercise Your Faith When You step into that water, test it out before you put your foot down. Feel the substance that your Faith provides Heb 11:1 Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen.
          • Prayer Leads Increased Faith Even the apostles prayed for more faith Luk 17:5 And the apostles said unto the Lord, Increase our faith.
          • Reading Your Bible This sets us apart and keeps us unspotted from the world Eph 5:25 Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church, and gave himself for it; Eph 5:26 That he might sanctify and cleanse it with the washing of water by the word,
          • Being Faithful to the house of God Heb 10:25 Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching.
        • Finally, Let Go Of The Ship If You are going to get victory and get off the boat you have to break free on the grip this world has on you Jas 4:4 Ye adulterers and adulteresses, know ye not that the friendship of the world is enmity with God? whosoever therefore will be a friend of the world is the enemy of God.

        Once Off The Boat, Stay Above Water

        • Keep You Eyes Upon Jesus At first Peter makes no mention of the storm. But somewhere along the line he stopped looking at God and started looking at the storm Mat 14:30 But when he saw the wind boisterous, he was afraid; and beginning to sink, he cried, saying, Lord, save me.
        • Get Your Eyes Back On Jesus Peter started sinking, and all he could think about was himself and the storm. If he would have turn to Jesus at that point with the same faith as before no doubt he would have quickly recovered Mat 14:30 But when he saw the wind boisterous, he was afraid; and beginning to sink, he cried, saying, Lord, save me.
          • Your Going To Stumble, when you do look to God for help
        • If that fails - Remember The Saviour Peter knew there was only one who could save him Mat 14:30 But when he saw the wind boisterous, he was afraid; and beginning to sink, he cried, saying, Lord, save me.
          • If You Fall, regroup and call upon Jesus Dont let mistakes keep you away from God, that is not excusable in His eyes. Humble yourself and seek forgiveness 1Jn 1:9 If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.
