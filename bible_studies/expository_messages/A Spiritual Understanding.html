Intro

  • How God Sees Us Last week we learned that God looks at us differently then God sees us Turn Your Bible to 1 Co 2:14
    • Natural or Spiritually Blind We are constantly being influenced by the spiritual things, but we have no real knowledge of them. We are spiritually blind 1Co 2:14  But the natural man receiveth not the things of the Spirit of God: for they are foolishness unto him: neither can he know them, because they are spiritually discerned. 
    • Carnal - Young Spiritual Vision Once you have accepted Christ into your heart, you have a new UNTRAINED spiritual vision 1Co_3:1  And I, brethren, could not speak unto you as unto spiritual, but as unto carnal, even as unto babes in Christ.
    • Spiritual - Walking With Godly Maturity 1Co 2:16  For who hath known the mind of the Lord, that he may instruct him? But we have the mind of Christ.
  • The Importance Of Spiritual God places great importance on spiritual matters The word Spiritual and Spirit are mentioned a total of 479 times in the Bible
    • Our Text: Col 1:16 We see here two planes of existance, those things that are physical (the visible) or those things that are invisible (the spiritual)
    • All Things Are By Him Col 1:17  And he is before all things, and by him all things consist.

  • Becoming Spiritual Is The Goal of Every Christian It is the target to be more minded on the higher things of God then of the things of this world Php 3:14  I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus. 

What Is Spiritual?

  • Consider These 3 Types of Spiritual Beings
  • The Spirit of Man The spirit of man is his soul
    • The Soul's Value The value of the soul is more important than anything the world has to offer, thats why no drug, no amount of money, no anything other than God can satisfy it Mat 16:26  For what is a man profited, if he shall gain the whole world, and lose his own soul? or what shall a man give in exchange for his soul? 
    • Soul is Only Effected Spiritually There are those that have been through serious trauma but live in absolute joy, thats because the world has no power of the soul, only spiritual matters affect it Mat 10:28  And fear not them which kill the body, but are not able to kill the soul: but rather fear him which is able to destroy both soul and body in hell. 
  • The Spirit of Wickedness Not all spiritual things are good. In fact, there is an enemy that exists in the spiritual realm that we must be on guard against. The enemy has always been there but we can see its opposition once the Spirit of God opens our eyes Eph 6:12  For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places. 
    • Once saved, opposition intesifies Eph 6:12 AGAINST principalities, AGAINST powers, AGAINST the rulers of the darkness of this world
    • Not Omniscient, but Can See Long Distances Eph 6:12 in HIGH PLACES.
  • The Spirit of God Joh 4:24  God is a Spirit: and they that worship him must worship him in spirit and in truth. 
    • Sons of God Are Led By Him Want to Identify with God, follow Him Rom 8:13  For if ye live after the flesh, ye shall die: but if ye through the Spirit do mortify the deeds of the body, ye shall live.  Rom 8:14  For as many as are led by the Spirit of God, they are the sons of God. 
    • His Spirit Speaks For Us God knows the deepest parts of your heart, and is constantly speaking to God and asking for those things you didnt even know you needed Rom 8:26  Likewise the Spirit also helpeth our infirmities: for we know not what we should pray for as we ought: but the Spirit itself maketh intercession for us with groanings which cannot be uttered. 
    • His Spirit Comforts Us Doesnt matter what you been through, there's nothing in your life God cant make better if you give it to Him Joh 14:26  But the Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father will send in my name, he shall teach you all things, and bring all things to your remembrance, whatsoever I have said unto you. 

What A Godly Spirit Does For You - Refer to Col 1:10-11

  • Enables A Worthy Walk Col 1:10  That ye might walk worthy of the Lord unto all pleasing,
    • Your Will Be Living In God's Purpose When you are walking with God, your goals align with His and He opens doors you never thought you would have access to
    • There Is Still A Decision To Make Being a Christian is all about making decisions for Him. He wants a willing heart - That ye MIGHT walk worthy - Col 1:10
    • God Will Be Pleased It means a lot to me that God gives the opportunity to be pleasing to Him. I know I bring nothing to the table, but God still allows me this opportunity unto all pleasing,
  • Gives Blessings of Victory If You are saved and struggling, ask this question: Are you in the Spirit of God? Col 1:10 being fruitful in every good work
  • You Will Know God I want to know God. and increasing in the knowledge of God; 
  • You Will Be Prepared For Battle Col 1:11  Strengthened with all might, according to his glorious power, unto all patience and longsuffering with joyfulness; 

How To Walk In The Spirit Of God?

  • If You Are Going To Receive His Blessings:
    • You Must First Be Saved It is the first step, before we are saved we are spiritually blind Col 1:14  In whom we have redemption through his blood, even the forgiveness of sins: 
    • We Must Submit To His Authority Col 1:16  For by him were all things created, that are in heaven, and that are in earth, visible and invisible, whether they be thrones, or dominions, or principalities, or powers: all things were created by him, and for him: 
    • We Must Be A Apart Of His Body Col 1:18  And he is the head of the body, the church: who is the beginning, the firstborn from the dead; that in all things he might have the preeminence. 
  • He Must Come First In Your Life Col 1:18 that in all things he might have the preeminence. 
    • Pre-eminence means He Has The Priority In Your Life Your problems and desires are not priority. Try Putting Him first and watch how these other things sort themselves out Mat 6:33  But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you.
