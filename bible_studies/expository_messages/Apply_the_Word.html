What To Do With His Word

  • Jas 1:19  Wherefore, my beloved brethren, let every man be swift to hear, slow to speak, slow to wrath: 
  • Be Swift To Hear It Youve heard it said that you have 2 ears and one mouth so that you are to listen twice as much. I say that this saying applies here. Listen to God's truth, hear it, be careful to consider it
  • Be Slow To Speak Many times when we are listening to someone we are forming our counter arguments in our mind. When it comes to the Word of God this behavior will destroy you and keep you from getting what God has for you. Dont drown out the miracle God has for you
  • Be Slow To Wrath When truly listening to the Word of God, the Holy Spirit will start working in you to point things out that is keeping you from God. Joh 16:7  Nevertheless I tell you the truth; It is expedient for you that I go away: for if I go not away, the Comforter will not come unto you; but if I depart, I will send him unto you.  Joh 16:8  And when he is come, he will reprove the world of sin, and of righteousness, and of judgment: 

What His Word Will Do For You

  • His Word Will Save You Dont let this go by without grabbing a hold of it. There is not a person in this room that does not benefit if God intervenes on their life. If something is trying to convince you otherwise, let it go! Jas 1:21  Wherefore lay apart all filthiness and superfluity of naughtiness, and receive with meekness the engrafted word, which is able to save your souls.
  • His Word Will Give You a Heart Transplant Im not exaggerating here, my life was different, I was a nothing sinner. Today I am not a something and I am still a sinner but I have a new life in me. The word engrafted in James 1:21 means an "organ transplant" - His Word grafted into your heart and receive with meekness the engrafted word, which is able to save your souls.

  • Psa 119:105  NUN. Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, and a light unto my path. 
  • It Reveals To Us Our Unrighteousness Have you ever met someone who thought they could do no wrong. Or has sin ever gotten you so deep that it felt normal. His Word shines a light right onto the whole mess to show us who we really are. Its painful, I admit. But if the gangrene on your foot is going to spread to the whole body, isnt it better to cut it off to save your life! Dont let it continue to fester, let the light shine onto your reality. I got news for you, no matter your reality, we are all unrighteous. There is not a sin youve commited that will keep you from the grace of God Rom 3:10  As it is written, There is none righteous, no, not one:
