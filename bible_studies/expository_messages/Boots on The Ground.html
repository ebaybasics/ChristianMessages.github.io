        What Do You Stand For?

        • The Call to Stand for Christ In a world that often stands against God, we are called to make a firm stand for Christ. Romans 12:2 (KJV), "And be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect, will of God."
          • CTA reflect on what areas in your life you are conforming to the world. Identify those areas and commit to standing for Christ in those spaces.
        • The Danger of Standing for Nothing "If you don't stand for something, you'll fall for anything." As believers, if we don't stand firm in our faith, we risk being tossed to and fro by every wind of doctrine, every new and enticing philosophy that the world presents Ephesians 4:14 (KJV) says, "That we henceforth be no more children, tossed to and fro, and carried about with every wind of doctrine, by the sleight of men, and cunning craftiness, whereby they lie in wait to deceive."
          • CTA What are the non-negotiables of your faith? Firmly establish these truths in your heart and be ready to defend them.
        • The Importance of Taking a Stand James 4:7 (KJV), we are told, "Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you."
          • CTA Stand up for justice, reach out in love, and live out your faith in a way that points others to Christ.
