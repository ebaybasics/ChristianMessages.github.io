    • Jesus Healed the Leper Peter seen through faith Jesus healing the leper (contagious diseased man) Mat 8:2  And, behold, there came a leper and worshipped him, saying, Lord, if thou wilt, thou canst make me clean. (Notice The Lepers Faith) Mat 8:3  And Jesus put forth his hand, and touched him, saying, I will; be thou clean. And immediately his leprosy was cleansed. 
    • Jesus Healed the Crippled Man Peter seen through faith... Mat 9:2  And, behold, they brought to him a man sick of the palsy, lying on a bed: and Jesus seeing their faith said unto the sick of the palsy; Son, be of good cheer; thy sins be forgiven thee. 
    • Jesus Gave The Blind Sight Peter seen through faith... Mat 9:28  And when he was come into the house, the blind men came to him: and Jesus saith unto them, Believe ye that I am able to do this? They said unto him, Yea, Lord.  Mat 9:29  Then touched he their eyes, saying, According to your faith be it unto you.

Getting Off The Boat

  • Listen To The Invitation When God is talking to you, make a decision to follow God's call on your life. Mat 14:29  And he said, Come.
  • Take A Step Towards God Dont just hear His invitation, Act on it. Draw Nigh to God. I can imagine as Peter was stepping toward God that God was stepping toward Peter Jas 4:8  Draw nigh to God, and he will draw nigh to you. Cleanse your hands, ye sinners; and purify your hearts, ye double minded. 
  • Increase Your Faith When You step into that water, test it out before you put your foot down. Feel the substance that your Faith provides Heb 11:1  Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen. 
    • Prayer Leads Increased Faith Even the apostles prayed for more faith Luk 17:5  And the apostles said unto the Lord, Increase our faith. 
    • Reading Your Bible This sets us apart and keeps us unspotted from the world Eph 5:25  Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church, and gave himself for it;  Eph 5:26  That he might sanctify and cleanse it with the washing of water by the word, 
    • Being Faithful to the house of God Heb 10:25  Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching. 
  • Let Go Of The Ship If You are going to get victory and get off the boat you have to break free on the grip this world has on you Jas 4:4  Ye adulterers and adulteresses, know ye not that the friendship of the world is enmity with God? whosoever therefore will be a friend of the world is the enemy of God. 

Staying Above Water

  • Keep You Eyes Upon Jesus At first Peter makes no mention of the storm. But somewhere along the line he stopped looking at God and started looking at the storm Mat 14:30  But when he saw the wind boisterous, he was afraid; and beginning to sink, he cried, saying, Lord, save me. 
  • Get Your Eyes Back On Jesus Peter started sinking, and all he could think about was himself and the storm. If he would have turn to Jesus at that point with the same faith as before no doubt he would have quickly recovered Mat 14:30  But when he saw the wind boisterous, he was afraid; and beginning to sink, he cried, saying, Lord, save me. 
    • Your Going To Stumble, when you do look to God for help
  • If that fails - Remember The Saviour Peter knew there was only one who could save him Mat 14:30  But when he saw the wind boisterous, he was afraid; and beginning to sink, he cried, saying, Lord, save me. 
    • If You Fall, regroup and call upon Jesus Dont let mistakes keep you away from God, that is not excusable in His eyes. Humble yourself and seek forgiveness 1Jn 1:9  If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.
