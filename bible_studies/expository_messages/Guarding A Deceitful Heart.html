If Our Heart Is Desperately Wicked, How Do We Trust In God With All Our Heart?

    Pro 3:5  Trust in the LORD with all thine heart; and lean not unto thine own understanding. 

    • On The Surface, It Seems Impossible And without God it is, but this is the miracle of Salvation. Once saved, God moves into your heart.
      • Eph 3:17  That Christ may dwell in your hearts by faith; that ye, being rooted and grounded in love, 
    • However, Once God Moves In Its Not Instantly "All Thine Heart" God takes up residence, but He leaves it up to us to follow after Him, to sanctify ourselves and give Him more of our hearts
    • Salvation and Sanctification Make You A Tree That is what it means to trust in the Lord and Make the Lord Your Hope (Turn Back To Jer 17:7) Jer 17:7  Blessed is the man that trusteth in the LORD, and whose hope the LORD is. 
    • If You Follow These 3 Principles With Respect To Your Heart You Will Grow Strong Roots

    1) Don't Follow, But Guide Your Heart

    • Treasure Eternity If youre saved this house is not your home. Start setting your minds eye on Heaven, and that moment you see Jesus with His arms outstretched Mat 6:21  For where your treasure is, there will your heart be also. 
    • Make God's House a Priority When you come to church you are pulling in nutrients from the ground and the water. This gives you unmovable roots Jer 17:8 ...planted by the waters, and that spreadeth out her roots by the river
    • Let People See The New Life God Has Given You When the world sees you, do they see a Christian, or would the be surprised to find out that you follow after God Jer 17:8 but her leaf shall be green;

    2) Guard Your Heart

    • Signs Your Heart Is Deceiving You Once saved God moves in, but your heart will still seek to decieve you into wickedness
      • Your Offended By The Preaching Of God's Word
      • You feel isolated
      • You deserve more This could be time to yourself, better treated, more respect etc. These are all pride issues
    • Be On Guard Externally We need our armor, its of God and He protects our heart from the wiles of the devil Eph 6:14  Stand therefore, having your loins girt about with truth, and having on the breastplate of righteousness; 
    • We Can Claim Our Victory Through Him Rom 8:37  Nay, in all these things we are more than conquerors through him that loved us. 

    3) Grow Your Heart

    • Continue in the Word Matthew 4:4, 'Man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word that proceedeth out of the mouth of God.
    • Its A Daily Commitment Luk 9:23  And he said to them all, If any man will come after me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross daily, and follow me. 
    • Jesus is the Only Way Joh 14:6  Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me. 

    Conclusion

    • Guiding Our Heart is Not Easy, But Its Critical As Jeremiah warns us, the heart is deceitful above all things, and desperately wicked. But this same passage also tells us of the immense blessings that come to those who place their trust and hope in the Lord.
