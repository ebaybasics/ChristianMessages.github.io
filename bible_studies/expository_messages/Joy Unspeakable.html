Checking The Temperature of Our Lives

  • The Speed of Life We live in a fast paced world today. We're continually reacting to external stimuli, making split-second decisions that affect our journey.
  • Emotional Rollercoaster As we navigate life, our emotions fluctuate dramatically, oscillating between happiness, irritation, sadness, and excitement.
  • The Importance of Pausing Just as a vehicle requires regular maintenance checks to prevent breakdowns, we too must periodically pause and assess our emotional and spiritual state.
  • Do You Find This Morning That You Have Joy Amidst the chaos, the true measure of our well-being is the consistent joy we experience, irrespective of our circumstances

What Is Joy?

  • Joy Defined: JOY is a delight of the mind, from the consideration of the present or assured approaching possession of a good. This definition teaches us that Joy is one of two things, 1) It is circumstantial or 2) It is anchored on Hope
