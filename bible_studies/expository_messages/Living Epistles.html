2Co 3:2  Ye are our epistle written in our hearts, known and read of all men:  2Co 3:3  Forasmuch as ye are manifestly declared to be the epistle of Christ ministered by us, written not with ink, but with the Spirit of the living God; not in tables of stone, but in fleshy tables of the heart. 

A group of atheists were criticizing the Bible. One of them spoke about creation. He said, "What man with any common sense could believe that several thousand years ago, God stooped down and picked up a piece of mud, breathed on it, and changed it into a human being?" A Christian man standing by overheard the conversation and responded by saying: "I cannot answer all the questions about creation, but this I know: One night God stooped down and picked up the dirtiest piece of mud in this city, breathed upon it by His Holy Spirit, and changed a gambling, thieving, drinking wretch into a peace-loving man of God. I was that man."

    I Thanks God that He is in the Business of Transforming Lives

    • He is in the Business of: Turning Hearts of Stone into Hearts of Flesh This world, and this flesh gives you some calluses and thick skin, but no matter how callused you are, deep down everyone of us longs to have life in our heart again.
      • God has what you are looking for: Eze 36:26  A new heart also will I give you, and a new spirit will I put within you: and I will take away the stony heart out of your flesh, and I will give you an heart of flesh. 
    • He is in the Business of: Converting Trials into Victories It's a tough time of the year to be without housing, without family, without hope. Give it to God this season, and watch God convert your test into a testimony James 1:2-3, "My brethren, count it all joy when ye fall into divers temptations; Knowing this, that the trying of your faith worketh patience."
    • He is in the Business of: Pulling people Out of the Darkness 1 Peter 2:9, "But ye are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, a peculiar people; that ye should shew forth the praises of him who hath called you out of darkness into his marvellous light."
    • My Question To You is This? Will you make the decision to surrender your will and your life over to the care of God this morning

    The Miracle of Surrender

    • Finding Freedom in Surrender You know that freedom youve been looking for? That freedom from misery, from cravings, from the chains of this world? God says you find that freedom in surrendering to Him. John 8:36, "If the Son therefore shall make you free, ye shall be free indeed."
      • Could it be that you are struggling because God is drawing you Paul understood this all to well when he himself was fighting God on that road to Damascus Act 9:5  And he said, Who art thou, Lord? And the Lord said, I am Jesus whom thou persecutest: it is hard for thee to kick against the pricks. 
    • Finding Peace Beyond Understanding We have a missionary to Kenya, Brother David Smith. He was telling me a story of how his newborn baby was in the hospital. The hospital was trying to prepare him for the worst case scenario. David went to his knees in prayer, and told God that the baby belonged to God. If it was God's will then he would be at peace with that. God's will was that baby would live and is happy and healthy today. This is an illustration of the peace God wants to give to you but can only come from fully trusting Him Philippians 4:7, "And the peace of God, which passeth all understanding, shall keep your hearts and minds through Christ Jesus."
    • Becoming a Living Epistle Through God’s Indwelling
      • An Epistle Is: A letter written to others. Much of the new testament is in the form of actual letters written by the Apostle Paul to various churches of the time.
      • You become a Living Testimony If you were telling someone about your life story right now, what would you say the purpose of your life has been. God says He has a purpose for you and His purpose is great. Jer 33:3  Call unto me, and I will answer thee, and shew thee great and mighty things, which thou knowest not. 

    How To Be One of God's Living Letters To The World

      A) His Letter's Are Written on Prepared Material

      • 1) We Must have a Cleansed and Renewed Heart Ever try to work in a cluttered workspace. -- How do we clean our hearts though? Thankfully we have the expert for that. Psalm 51:10, "Create in me a clean heart, O God; and renew a right spirit within me."
        • Salvation is First
        • Sometimes a Saved Man Falls 1 John 1:9 - If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.
      • 2) We Must Start Seperating From The World You know what the world has to offer, if you want more of that than it will be there for you to take. Why not seperate yourself from the source of all the misery and draw closer to God. Romans 12:2, "And be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind...
      • 3) Once the Foundation is Laid it's Time to Start Building Many there is who are saved and have a foundation of stone laid but never begin to build. Matthew 7:24, "Therefore whosoever heareth these sayings of mine, and doeth them, I will liken him unto a wise man, which BUILT his house upon a rock.

      B) His Letter's Reveal His Character

      • 1) We Must Love His Church When people see us, they should know where we are going to be on Sunday morning. Because the God that lives within us that are born-again, loves His church, and gave His life for it. John 13:35, "By this shall all men know that ye are my disciples, if ye have love one to another."
      • 2) We Must Walk With Him My son walks to school, but he always waits for his friend because he wants to walk with him. I wonder, do we find ourselves wanting to walk with God in this way? 1 John 2:6, "He that saith he abideth in him ought himself also so to walk, even as he walked."
      • 3) We Must Serve As Him Luke 22:27: "For whether is greater, he that sitteth at meat, or he that serveth? is not he that sitteth at meat? but I am among you as he that serveth."

      C) His Letter's Have His Signature

      • 1) Is Eternity Written On Our Hearts Once we are His, we have been given an eternal promise. The hope that others are looking for lives inside of us. And that hope is eternally sealed for all believers Ephesians 1:13, "In whom ye also trusted, after that ye heard the word of truth, the gospel of your salvation: in whom also after that ye believed, ye were sealed with that holy Spirit of promise."
      • 2) His Signature Comes With Authority You know those things that we hate so much because we cant get victory over them? For me it was drug addiction. But when I got saved I got power over the world, even those addictions that held me in bondage 1 John 5:4-5: "For whatsoever is born of God overcometh the world: and this is the victory that overcometh the world, even our faith. Who is he that overcometh the world, but he that believeth that Jesus is the Son of God?"
