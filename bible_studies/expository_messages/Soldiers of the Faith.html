Soldiers of the Faith

2 Timothy 2:1-4 "Thou therefore, my son, be strong in the grace that is in Christ Jesus. And the things that thou hast heard of me among many witnesses, the same commit thou to faithful men, who shall be able to teach others also. Thou therefore endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ. No man that warreth entangleth himself with the affairs of this life; that he may please him who hath chosen him to be a soldier."

Recap:

  • Last week: We talked about the difference between being born again and being a Christian - Not all saved people can call themselves Christians Mat 16:24-25 Then said Jesus unto his disciples, If any man will come after me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross, and follow me. For whosoever will save his life shall lose it: and whosoever will lose his life for my sake shall find it.
  • Soldiers of Christ Those that choose to be Christians are God's chosen soldiers A good soldier has these characteristics:
    • Trustworthy Your word is your bond A good soldier can be counted on on the battlefield
    • Courageous Willing to move against heavy enemy fire without backing down
    • Respectful of Your Brothers We all have different personalities and different talents. In the battle, we need to remember we have the same goal
    • Conviction of Purpose You're never going to make it to the finish line if you don't believe in what you're fighting for. A good soldier that can endure hardness will believe in what he is fighting for

Good Soldiers Need Strength: 2Ti 2:1 Thou therefore, my son, be strong in the grace that is in Christ Jesus.

  • Be Ready for Opposition When you are saved out of the fire, you get a target on your back. You ever notice how when your life is starting to come together, that's when the devil shows up. 1Pe 5:8 Be sober, be vigilant; because your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour:
  • Need a Strong Footing You can't lose your salvation, but the devil will do everything it can to make you unusable for the cause of Christ. You are in the grace, but to be usable by God, you need to walk in the Spirit. Gal 5:25 If we live in the Spirit, let us also walk in the Spirit.
    • Fruit of the Spirit: Gal 5:22 But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness, goodness, faith, Gal 5:23 Meekness, temperance: against such there is no law.
  • Need a Strong Grip Grab a hold of the promises of God. God gives us a model of how to do this in Malachi Mal 3:10 Bring ye all the tithes into the storehouse, that there may be meat in mine house, and prove me now herewith, saith the LORD of hosts, if I will not open you the windows of heaven, and pour you out a blessing, that there shall not be room enough to receive it.
  • Need a Strong Backbone Every week I ask that you stand when singing in the sanctuary. Usually, everyone will listen to the first song, then the second song 1 person will sit. Then those that are weak of conviction will follow suit. If you are going to be a good soldier, however, you need to stand even when others aren't Pro 1:10 My son, if sinners entice thee, consent thou not.

Good Soldiers Need To Be Faithful 2Ti 2:2 And the things that thou hast heard of me among many witnesses, the same commit thou to faithful men, who shall be able to teach others also.

  • God has Called Us To Look Find Faithful Men Faithful Men Are Hard To Find Pro 20:6 Most men will proclaim every one his own goodness: but a faithful man who can find?
  • Define Faithful Webster's 1828 - Just a few definitions:
    • Firmly in Adherence to the Truth You must be so firmly grounded in the truth that no matter what crazy philosophies someone tries to sell you on, you're unmovable. Col 2:7 Rooted and built up in him, and stablished in the faith, as ye have been taught, abounding therein with thanksgiving.
    • Loyal You're going to have a hard time convincing someone you love God if you turn your back on Him every chance you get. Jas 1:8 A double-minded man is unstable in all his ways.
    • Constant in the Performance of Duties Are You a Good Soldier? Are you faithful to the house of God? That means are you regularly attending? Heb 10:25 Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching.
  • Why Be Faithful? God Wants To Train You and Trust You with His Mysteries: Our Text: And the things that thou hast heard of me...the same commit thou to faithful men
  • He Wants You To Carry Out His Training To Others Our Text: who shall be able to teach others also.

Good Soldiers Need To Be Prepared 2Ti 2:3 Thou therefore endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ.

  • You Are Called To Endure Hardness Mat 7:24 Therefore whosoever heareth these sayings of mine, and doeth them, I will liken him unto a wise man, which built his house upon a rock: Mat 7:25 And the rain descended, and the floods came, and the winds blew, and beat upon that house; and it fell not: for it was founded upon a rock. Mat 7:26 And every one that heareth these sayings of mine, and doeth them not, shall be likened unto a foolish man, which built his house upon the sand: Mat 7:27 And the rain descended, and the floods came, and the winds blew, and beat upon that house; and it fell: and great was the fall of it.
    • Everyone faces storms in their life No doubt you have faced hard times. The difference is, what is your house built on?

How To Endure Hardness

  • You Must Be Born Again A lot of people profess to be saved, almost just as many can quote you some verses, but is Christ in your heart? If you've never felt compelled to follow after Christ of your own desire, you should question your own salvation. Why? Because it is the single most important decision you will ever make. Can you go to a time in your mind when you called out on Jesus, confessed you were a sinner that needed saving, and believed He was the only one capable of doing it? When you are reborn, you are battleborn Joh 3:7 Marvel not that I said unto thee, Ye must be born again.
  • Join Alongside Fellow Soldiers You want to endure hardness, we are called to do it together, side by side Gal 6:2 Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ.
  • Stay in Communication With The Commander God has given you direct access to the throne, use it
    • Speak To Him Through Prayer Jas 1:5 If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him.
    • Listen To Him Through Reading The Bible
    • Apply His Truth To Your Life Jas 1:22 But be ye doers of the word, and not hearers only, deceiving your own selves.
  • Get Involved With His Ministries
  • Find Faithful Men To Train Closing the circle of our text: 2Ti 2:2 And the things that thou hast heard of me among many witnesses, the same commit thou to faithful men, who shall be able to teach others also.

This Leads To Victory

  • Complicated Lives Become Simple When your focus is on the purposeful battle, God works out all the other junk in your life. Try putting Him first for a while and watch how your worldly problems sort themselves out 2Ti 2:4 No man that warreth entangleth himself with the affairs of this life; that he may please him who hath chosen him to be a soldier.
  • Doors Start Opening For You Are you worried about a place to stay? Put God first. Are you worried about your next meal? Put God first. Are you worried about a job? Put God first. Put God first and see if He doesn't fulfill His promise to provide for you. Start trusting Him with your sustenance Mat 6:33 But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you.
  • God Has a Calling For Your Life You want to know what your purpose is? Seek Him, be a good soldier, and press toward the mark that is in Christ. Your life will be filled with more purpose than you know what to do with: Php 3:14 I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus.

Closing:

  • Lost: If you don't know Christ, that moment in time when you surrendered it all to Him, do so today. God wants to use you, to lift you up to higher places. You are one decision away from having a whole 'nother life story.
  • Backslidden: If you have drifted away from God, come back to Him. He wants a relationship with you, He has so much more for you. A peace and a victory beyond the world's understanding
  • Christian: We need to stay the course. Christ has done so much for us, what more can we do for His Kingdom? It may seem insignificant or pointless sometimes, but every little thing we do accounts to His Honor and Glory, and it will be added to our account
