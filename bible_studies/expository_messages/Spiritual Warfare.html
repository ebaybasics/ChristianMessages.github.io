Spiritual Warfare

Eph 6:10  Finally, my brethren, be strong in the Lord, and in the power of his might.  Eph 6:11  Put on the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil.  Eph 6:12  For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places. 

Intro - All Things Spiritual Recap (Week 3)

  • One of the most misunderstood concepts Is Spirituality The natural man cannot understand the things of the Spiritual 1Co 2:14  But the natural man receiveth not the things of the Spirit of God: for they are foolishness unto him: neither can he know them, because they are spiritually discerned. 
  • Remember There are Two Planes of Existence:
    • Physical Existence (The natural) Unfortunately many will never understand more than the physical 1Co 15:44  It is sown a natural body; it is raised a spiritual body. There is a natural body, and there is a spiritual body. 
    • Spiritual Existence The goal of every Christian should be to have more Spiritual discernment 1Co 2:15  But he that is spiritual judgeth all things, yet he himself is judged of no man. 
  • It Is The Spirit of Man God Cares About "But the Lord said unto Samuel, Look not on his countenance, or on the height of his stature; because I have refused him: for the Lord seeth not as man seeth; for man looketh on the outward appearance, but the Lord looketh on the heart."
  • Once Saved, The Holy Spirit of God Moves In And Opens Our Eyes Eph 1:17  That the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of glory, may give unto you the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of him:  Eph 1:18  The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that ye may know what is the hope of his calling, and what the riches of the glory of his inheritance in the saints, 
  • God Calls Us To Walk In His Spirit If We dont we become spiritually dehydrated, and exposed to attack Gal 5:16  This I say then, Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh. 
  • Once Our Eyes Are Opened, We Can See The Spiritual Battlefield The Bible Teaches that there is an enemy, the devil, and his goal is to use us as instruments to attack all things Holy Rom 6:13  Neither yield ye your members as instruments of unrighteousness unto sin: but yield yourselves unto God, as those that are alive from the dead, and your members as instruments of righteousness unto God. 

The Strategies of The Enemy

  • Eph 6:11 "The Wiles of the devil."
    • Definition: WILE: A trick or stratagem practiced for ensnaring or deception; a sly, insidious artifice.
    • Illustration: The Media The media is good at getting you to look at one thing when really the thing you should be looking at is elsewhere: BLM etc
  • Strategy 1) The Devil Does Not Exist If the devil doesnt exist, God doesnt exist. 2Co 11:14  And no marvel; for Satan himself is transformed into an angel of light. 
    • Illustration: The religion of Science (angel of light)
  • Strategy 2) That we are each others enemies The Bible teaches us who the enemy is Eph 6:12  For we wrestle not against flesh and blood,
  • Strategy 3) Incremental Sin (One of the most successful campaigns) The idea that sin is not that serious. If we will allow a little sin thats not such a big deal, then where do we draw the line. Mat 24:12  And because iniquity shall abound, the love of many shall wax cold. 

Signs You Are Under Spiritual Attack - And How To Back On Track

  • Everything Seems To Go Wrong The devil will put up stumbling blocks, but through Christ you can overcome them Rom 8:28  And we know that all things work together for good to them that love God, to them who are the called according to his purpose. 
    • Child Support Illustration I thought the world was going to take me out
  • Guilt of The Past The devil will try to convict you of sins you have already been pardoned for Psa 103:12  As far as the east is from the west, so far hath he removed our transgressions from us. 
  • Feelings of Insecurities Around The Brethren The devil would love for you to feel like you dont belong in the house of God Mar 2:17  When Jesus heard it, he saith unto them, They that are whole have no need of the physician, but they that are sick: I came not to call the righteous, but sinners to repentance. 
  • The Wrong Thoughts Entering Your Mind Its not a sin to have a bad thought, its a sin to dwell on it. Even the apostle paul had struggles with his mind 2Co 10:5  Casting down imaginations, and every high thing that exalteth itself against the knowledge of God, and bringing into captivity every thought to the obedience of Christ; 

How To Handle The Battle

    1) Put On Your Spiritual Armor

    • You Must Be Saved Until you are saved you are just an instrument of unrighteousness. You must yield yourselves to Him Eph 6:10  Finally, my brethren,
    • Choose the Hill You Stand On You can be strong for the wrong things. At some point you must make a decision, what do you stand for? Eph 6:10 Be strong in the Lord
    • You Must Be Led By Him Being saved is just the begininning. You must give Him control to feel His strength and power in your life Eph: 6:10 and in the power of his might. 
    • Its Your Decision Christ never forces His will, You must choose to ready yourself. Eph 6:11  Put on the whole armour of God,
    • Leave Nothing Vulnerable The one point you leave open will be the point where you are attacked Eph 6:11 the whole armour of God

    2) Be A Member Of The Church

    • The Church is Where You Find Refuge Out there, its a battle, In here is where you find a break from it all. Psa 91:2  I will say of the LORD, He is my refuge and my fortress: my God; in him will I trust. 
    • The Church Is Where You Receive Training In church you get Spiritually fed, and prepared for whats out there. Thats why its important to go to a church that doesnt hold back the truth just to tickle your ears!!! Pro 27:17  Iron sharpeneth iron; so a man sharpeneth the countenance of his friend. 
    • The Church Is Where You Find Healing Jas 5:14  Is any sick among you? let him call for the elders of the church; and let them pray over him, anointing him with oil in the name of the Lord: 

    Can I Ask You

    • Are You Battle Born Have your eyes ever been opened to the Spiritual things of this world?
    • Has The Battle Worn You Out? God Says Come To Him Mat 11:28  Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest. 
    • Are You Battle Ready? Maybe God is Calling You to Serve, Allow yourself to be led by Him Rom 8:14  For as many as are led by the Spirit of God, they are the sons of God.
