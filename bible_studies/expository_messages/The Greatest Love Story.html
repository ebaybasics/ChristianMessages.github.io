              • 1. The Unfailing Love of Our Creator Brethren, whether you find yourself saved or still seeking, know this: the cornerstone of our faith, the first attribute of the Fruit of the Spirit, is Love. Divine love calls us all, from every station in life, even from the darkest corners and hardest benches of the street. God desires to love us, and for us to extend that love unto others. His love is universal, not confined to a building or a social class. So, I say unto you, His love is for every soul here.

So let us be His hands and feet to those less fortunate, reflecting His unwavering love in a world desperately in need of it.
