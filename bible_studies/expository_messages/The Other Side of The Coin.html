The Other Side of The Coin

Mat 8:19-20 And a certain scribe came, and said unto him, Master, I will follow thee whithersoever thou goest. Mat 8:20 And Jesus saith unto him, The foxes have holes, and the birds of the air have nests; but the Son of man hath not where to lay his head.

Message Title: The Other Side Of Christianity

    What We are Used To Hearing: Many teachers and preachers will stand behind pulpits and tell you to call upon Jesus and follow after Him because:

    • Your Sins Will Be Forgiven We've all heard this before, call upon the name of Christ and your sins will be washed away.
      • Of course the Bible does say: Eph 1:7 In whom we have redemption through his blood, the forgiveness of sins, according to the riches of his grace;
    • You Will Have Peace In Your Life Mat 11:28 Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest.
    • That God Will Always Be With You: Heb 13:5 Let your conversation be without covetousness; and be content with such things as ye have: for he hath said, I will never leave thee, nor forsake thee.

    But There Is More To The Story: Where all these statements are Biblically sound truths, by telling you that and nothing else you’re only getting half the picture

    • Following after Christ is Difficult In fact in our passage of scripture the scribe came to Jesus and said that he would follow Him wherever He went. In which Jesus replied, I don’t even have a place to lay my head at night. Mat 8:20 And Jesus saith unto him, The foxes have holes, and the birds of the air have nests; but the Son of man hath not where to lay his head.
      • There will be no parade Joh 15:18 If the world hate you, ye know that it hated me before it hated you.
      • Dont expect to be famous Joh 15:19 If ye were of the world, the world would love his own: but because ye are not of the world, but I have chosen you out of the world, therefore the world hateth you.
      • There is no Fortune Consider the apostles
    • God's Training Is Difficult Not only will the world hate you and persecute you but God's training will push you to your limits Eph 2:10  For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus unto good works, which God hath before ordained that we should walk in them. 
      • Christians are Tried With Fire It should be the goal of every Christian to be more like Christ, and that is not an easy process because of our flesh 1Pe 1:7  That the trial of your faith, being much more precious than of gold that perisheth, though it be tried with fire, might be found unto praise and honour and glory at the appearing of Jesus Christ: 
      • Christians Are Pushed Out Of Their Comfort Zones Nothing grows in its comfort zone Illustration: soil depletion from being over cultivated  leads to the nutrients being depleted (in the comfort zone)
    • Being A Christian Is The Most Important Decision You Will Ever Make Its more important than where you live, what car you drive or how much money you have.
      • It's not Easy, But It Is Worth It I do not exaggerate when I say this is a game for keeps, a matter of life or death Mat 16:26  For what is a man profited, if he shall gain the whole world, and lose his own soul? or what shall a man give in exchange for his soul? 

    Becoming A Christian Through Biblical Example

      Turn Your Bible To Exodus 3:1 "Exo 3:1 Now Moses kept the flock of Jethro his father in law, the priest of Midian: and he led the flock to the backside of the desert, and came to the mountain of God, even to Horeb. "

      • There Was Nothing Special About Moses We read here that Moses was living his best blue collar life. The American dream. He was married and had a job working for his father in law. It wasnt because Moses was special in any kind of way that God would use him for such a task, but it was his willingness to follow
        • Let's see how this process unfolded:
      • 1) God Got Moses' Attention Exo 3:2 And the angel of the LORD appeared unto him in a flame of fire out of the midst of a bush: and he looked, and, behold, the bush burned with fire, and the bush was not consumed.
      • 2) Moses Made A Decision To Turn Toward God Exo 3:3 And Moses said, I will now turn aside, and see this great sight, why the bush is not burnt.
        • Moses Was Going His Way, Then Turned To God's This is the definition of a Godly repentance

      Hold Your Spot In Exodus 3 and Turn To Ephesians 2:8 : Eph 2:8  For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God:  Eph 2:9  Not of works, lest any man should boast. 

      • Salvation Comes Through Grace Notice, Moses didnt do anything to make God appear, God was there and God took all the necessary steps to bridge a relationship with Moses Eph 2:8  For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God: 
      • Salvation is Not Of Yourself This is important to grab ahold of. There is nothing you can do to earn salvation. No works will get you there. It is a gift Rom 6:23  For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord. 
      • What Are Some Works That People Think Will Get You To Heaven? Many false religions have sprung up out of Christianity because of this fallacy. The want to believe that there is something they must do to earn their place in heaven
        • Be A Good Person Islam believes if you do good deeds you will earn your place in heaven Rom 3:10  As it is written, There is none righteous, no, not one: 
        • Read A Lot Of Books Hinduism believes that through knowledge you can break the cycle of reincarnation
        • Baptism Baptism is a works, and unfortunately many so-called Christian churches will claim baptism is required for salvation Eph 2:9  Not of works, lest any man should boast. 
      • Salvation Comes From Trusting In The Lord Alone It's simple, and I know that we want to make it more complicated but God did all the work for us when He went to the cross Rom 10:9  That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved.  Rom 10:10  For with the heart man believeth unto righteousness; and with the mouth confession is made unto salvation. 

      Turn Back To Exodus 3

      • 3) God revealed Himself to Moses Remember, before we are born again we cannot see the Spiritual things of God. Once Saved we have Spiritual Vision Exo 3:4 And when the LORD saw that he turned aside to see, God called unto him out of the midst of the bush, and said, Moses, Moses. And he said, Here am I.
      • 4) God Prepared His Servant For Battle We call this discipleship, its educating you on the foundations God has set forth for us to follow Exo 3:5 And he said, Draw not nigh hither: put off thy shoes from off thy feet, for the place whereon thou standest is holy ground.
      • 5) God Pushed Moses Out Of His Comfort Zone Exo 3:10 Come now therefore, and I will send thee unto Pharaoh, that thou mayest bring forth my people the children of Israel out of Egypt.

      Moses's Response To God

      • When God Called Moses He Got Insecure Exo 3:11 And Moses said unto God, Who am I, that I should go unto Pharaoh, and that I should bring forth the children of Israel out of Egypt?
      • Moses Was Under Spiritual Attack and Moses Started:
        • 1) Remembering All Of His Sins Moses was a murderer, certainly God wouldnt call upon a murder for this Holy Task Exo 2:11 And it came to pass in those days, when Moses was grown, that he went out unto his brethren, and looked on their burdens: and he spied an Egyptian smiting an Hebrew, one of his brethren. Exo 2:12 And he looked this way and that way, and when he saw that there was no man, he slew the Egyptian, and hid him in the sand.
        • 2) Considered His Inadequacy Exo 4:10 And Moses said unto the LORD, O my Lord, I am not eloquent, neither heretofore, nor since thou hast spoken unto thy servant: but I am slow of speech, and of a slow tongue.
        • 3) Crisis Building Exo 4:1 And Moses answered and said, But, behold, they will not believe me, nor hearken unto my voice: for they will say, The LORD hath not appeared unto thee.
        • The Devil Was On Attack The devil wants us to believe that our past mistakes will hold us back, that are short comings will keep us from being successful, or that we will do everything right and still will not be good enough. This is what spiritual warfare looks like.

      God's Response To Moses

      • 1) Certainly I Am With You Exo 3:12 And he said, Certainly I will be with thee;
      • 2) God Was Planning The Victory Celebration Moses was worried about the logistics while God already was saying what would happen afterwards Exo 3:12 When thou hast brought forth the people out of Egypt, ye shall serve God upon this mountain.
        • There is someone in this room struggling with doubt or with insecurities of “what if” and God is already focused on your victory. Trust Him
      • 3) It Is God Sending You: Exo 3:14 And God said unto Moses, I AM THAT I AM: and he said, Thus shalt thou say unto the children of Israel, I AM hath sent me unto you.
      • 4) 3) You have all you that you need already: Exo 4:2 And the LORD said unto him, What is that in thine hand? And he said, A rod.
        • Stop Looking At What You Dont Have Going For You a. You have all that you need to be used by God right now, Don’t look at what you don’t have

      Closing:

      • This Christian life is not easy. In fact it is one of the most difficult things you will ever be a part of. There is no fortune or fame that comes with it, Even Jesus had no place to lay His head while He walked on this earth. It is worth it though. We have what the world is looking for. We have peace, we have unexplainable victories like Moses, and we have purpose.
      • 1) Maybe youre sitting there this morning saying you cant be used by God because you never trusted Him. You never turned to Him like Moses did that day at the burning bush. This is your time to turn to Him, God is trying to get your attention
      • 2) Are your insecurities keeping you from serving God like you should? You have everything you need to be useful for God right now, don’t let what you don’t have keep you from serving Him. Remember, He is God
      • 3) Truth is, none of us trust God like we ought to. It’s easy to focus on the what ifs, while God is already planning our victory celebration. Let us pray that God will increase our Faith to where He would have it be
