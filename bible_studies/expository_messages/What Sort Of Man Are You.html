Intro

  • In the Old Testament Days Jewish people classified men into two types: Jew or Gentile
  • Through Christ's Shed Blood, Gentiles Were Grafted In Paul Says It This Way: Eph 2:13  But now in Christ Jesus ye who sometimes were far off are made nigh by the blood of Christ.  Eph 2:14  For he is our peace, who hath made both one, and hath broken down the middle wall of partition between us; 
  • Jesus Opened The Door For All Men 2Pe 3:9  The Lord is not slack concerning his promise, as some men count slackness; but is longsuffering to us-ward, not willing that any should perish, but that all should come to repentance. 
  • The World Classifies Men In Many Types:
    • They Will Call Each Other: So Called Good Men, Family Men, Convicts, Drug Addicts, Homeless Men
    • God Looks At Us A Little Differently Than Men His Ways Are Higher Than Ours Thankfully Isa 55:8  For my thoughts are not your thoughts, neither are your ways my ways, saith the LORD.  Isa 55:9  For as the heavens are higher than the earth, so are my ways higher than your ways, and my thoughts than your thoughts. 

In God's Eyes, What Sort Of Man Our You?

  • Are You A Natural Man? Ever Since Adam Disobeyed God we are all born into the state of a natural man Rom 5:12  Wherefore, as by one man sin entered into the world, and death by sin; and so death passed upon all men, for that all have sinned: 
    • We Are All Wretched Sinners From The Start Rom 3:10  As it is written, There is none righteous, no, not one: 
  • The Natural Man Is Unsaved An unsaved man is an enemy of God Mat 12:30  He that is not with me is against me; and he that gathereth not with me scattereth abroad. 
  • Natural Man Is Without The Spirit Of God Illustration: Man reading the Bible and getting nothing out of it. 1Co 2:14  But the natural man receiveth not the things of the Spirit of God: for they are foolishness unto him: neither can he know them, because they are spiritually discerned. 
  • We All Have An Internal GPS But as we feed the flesh, this conscious of ours gets blurred Consider the progression of someone using drugs / Always changing morals as drug use progresses
  • Let Your Situation Be an Indicator If things are not going right for you, are you His? At Any Point You Can Become One of His
    • He Will never Leave You Heb 13:5  Let your conversation be without covetousness; and be content with such things as ye have: for he hath said, I will never leave thee, nor forsake thee. 
    • Answered Prayers Joh 9:31  Now we know that God heareth not sinners: but if any man be a worshipper of God, and doeth his will, him he heareth. 

Are You A Carnal Man?

  • A New Christian Is Reborn And like a newborn, they cant handle a Man's diet. 1Co 3:1  And I, brethren, could not speak unto you as unto spiritual, but as unto carnal, even as unto babes in Christ.  1Co 3:2  I have fed you with milk, and not with meat: for hitherto ye were not able to bear it, neither yet now are ye able.  1Co 3:3  For ye are yet carnal: for whereas there is among you envying, and strife, and divisions, are ye not carnal, and walk as men?  1Co 3:4  For while one saith, I am of Paul; and another, I am of Apollos; are ye not carnal? 
  • A Carnal Man Has An All About Me Christianity Mindset In the beginning that makes sense, if youre like me your life is a bit of a mess. But as you walk with God you should be growing, walking closer with God and having the heart of God.
  • A Carnal Man Still Is Trying To Keep His Old Self When you are saved you become a new creation. 2Co 5:17  Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new. 
    • Once Saved Always Saved But Living For God is a daily choice Eph 4:22  That ye put off concerning the former conversation the old man, which is corrupt according to the deceitful lusts; 
  • What Keeps Us Carnal? Often what keeps us from growing with Christ is thinking of ourselves higher than should  
    • Be Humble Rom 12:3  For I say, through the grace given unto me, to every man that is among you, not to think of himself more highly than he ought to think; but to think soberly, according as God hath dealt to every man the measure of faith. 
    • Just A Self Lie Gal 6:3  For if a man think himself to be something, when he is nothing, he deceiveth himself. 

Are You A Spiritual Man?

  • A Spiritual Man Desires the Things God Desires:
    • Desires A Holy-Seperated Life When the world looks at us, they should not see a reflection of themselves Joh 15:18  If the world hate you, ye know that it hated me before it hated you.  Joh 15:19  If ye were of the world, the world would love his own: but because ye are not of the world, but I have chosen you out of the world, therefore the world hateth you. 
    • Desires To Be With Fellow Christians Heb 10:25  Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching. 
    • Desires to Follow After Christ Joh 14:15  If ye love me, keep my commandments. 
  • A Spiritual Man Serves It bothers me when a Christian is not interested in ministry, or carrying others burdens Gal 6:1  Brethren, if a man be overtaken in a fault, ye which are spiritual, restore such an one in the spirit of meekness; considering thyself, lest thou also be tempted.  Gal 6:2  Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ. 

How To Be Spiritual? Turn to Galatians 5:16

  • We Must Walk In The Spirit Gal 5:16-17 That old flesh is keeping us away from God, but God says Walking with Him gives us victory over the flesh Gal 5:16  This I say then, Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh.  Gal 5:17  For the flesh lusteth against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh: and these are contrary the one to the other: so that ye cannot do the things that ye would. 
  • Get More Involved Sunday mornings are for your spiritual health, but it doesnt stop there:
    • Invite people to church God loves soul winners
    • Be The Light The world is tired of hypocrites. When people look at you they should see a difference
    • Read the Bible Be ready when someone asks questions 2Ti 4:2  Preach the word; be instant in season, out of season; reprove, rebuke, exhort with all longsuffering and doctrine. 
    • Join The Church The church is the Body of Christ, Join it and serve with us. Col 1:18  And he is the head of the body, the church: who is the beginning, the firstborn from the dead; that in all things he might have the preeminence. 

In Closing - What Sort OF Man Are You?

  • Have You Been Born Again? It doesnt matter if you go to church, or live right by the worlds standards. You must accept Christ into your heart and be born again
  • Are You growing in the faith? We all start at the same place, but are you growing? Are you receiving God's blessings but not following Him. Draw nigh to Him
  • Can You go Further? Christians, God has done so much for us. Is there something in your life God is dealing with you about. Allow God to have His perfect way with your life
