Jesus the Life

  • Recap The last couple of ways we considered Jesus the way, Jesus the truth, and this week we are looking at Jesus the Life Joh 14:6 Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me.
  • Jesus Offers a Life With Purpose No more wandering aimlessly. Everyday you wake up, you have something to aim for. That is to be pleasing to God and await His return Our Text: 1Th 1:10 "And to wait for his Son from heaven, "
    • Blessings of waiting on the Lord You will not be weighed down with the cares of this world. People will wonder where your strength comes from Isa_40:31 But they that wait upon the LORD shall renew their strength; they shall mount up with wings as eagles; they shall run, and not be weary; and they shall walk, and not faint.
  • His Life Gives Life When you receive Jesus as the Way and the Truth you receive a new life. Those things youve done, the things that keep you from movin on, are forgiven and gone! Our Text: 1Th 1:10 "whom he raised from the dead, " 2Co_5:17 Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new.
  • His Life Delivers Us From His Wrath When God has done so much for us those that reject Him will answer to Him. But us that accept God's way, we are innocent in God's eyes Our Text: 1Th 1:10 "even Jesus, which delivered us from the wrath to come."

What The World Sees

  • A Working Faith A man that is hot can put on a coat. Him putting on the coat does not make him cold and to most it will be obvious he is uncomfortable and truly does not need the coat. But to those that are cold, they put on a coat because they are cold. In the same way we can put in work for God without being born again, but it is like putting on a coat when you are hot, and just as obvious to the world. When you are born again, you want to get busy about the work of the Lord Jas 2:18 Yea, a man may say, Thou hast faith, and I have works: shew me thy faith without thy works, and I will shew thee my faith by my works. Our Text: 1Th 1:3 Remembering without ceasing your work of faith,
