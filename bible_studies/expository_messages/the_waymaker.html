      In Order For God to Intervene...

    • We Must Be Broken (the word poor in our text means to be of a humbled spirit) Our Text: "He raiseth up the poor out of the dust,"
    • Know Our Way Is Wrong (it may seem right but our current situation is a compass) Pro 14:12  There is a way which seemeth right unto a man, but the end thereof are the ways of death. 
    • Be Ready to cry out to God (We have to hit our own rock bottom) Psa 142:5-7  I cried unto thee, O LORD: I said, Thou art my refuge and my portion in the land of the living.  Attend unto my cry; for I am brought very low: deliver me from my persecutors; for they are stronger than I.  Bring my soul out of prison, that I may praise thy name: the righteous shall compass me about; for thou shalt deal bountifully with me. 
    • Then God Can Intervene (when we are in God's way, He can't help us) Psa_145:14  The LORD upholdeth all that fall, and raiseth up all those that be bowed down.

    • Change your Playmates (Who needs enemies with friends like so-n-so) Psa 68:6  God setteth the solitary in families: he bringeth out those which are bound with chains: but the rebellious dwell in a dry land.

      The Way To Receive His Promise of Intervention

    • Know He Is The Only Way (the world tries to offer many different solutions, AA, NA, etc but...) Joh 14:6  Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me. 
    • Know what separates us from God: (God desires a relationship with us, but we broke fellowship) Rom 6:23a   For the wages of sin is death;
    • Know He gave His life for ours: (In order to restore our relationship, Christ gave Himself) Rom 6:23b but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord.
    • Have a change in heart about our ways (We must realize our way is wrong and His way is right) Mat 4:17 “From that time Jesus began to preach, and to say, Repent: for the kingdom of heaven is at hand. “
    • To receive God's promised intervention, we must accept God's gift:(waiting then walking away as they hand us our gift)Rom 10:9  That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved.
