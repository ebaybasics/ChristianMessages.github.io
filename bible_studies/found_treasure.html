Finding God's Treasure

The Parable of the Hidden Treasure

Matthew 13:44: "Again, the kingdom of heaven is like unto treasure hid in a field; the which when a man hath found, he hideth, and for joy thereof goeth and selleth all that he hath, and buyeth that field."

Intro: Why are you here?

What is the treasure? (the goal, purpose, truth)

  • The treasure is peace: Mat 11:28 “Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest.”
  • The treasure is purpose: Philippians 3:14 “I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus.”
  • The treasure is privilege: Romans 6:18, "Being then made free from sin, ye became the servants of righteousness."

How to find the treasure?

  • Seek Him: Mat 6:33 But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you.
  • Remember who you are : Rom 5:8 But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us.
  • Desire to change your path: Mat 4:17 “From that time Jesus began to preach, and to say, Repent: for the kingdom of heaven is at hand. “
  • Give your life to Him: Rom 10:9 “That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved.”

Results of Finding the Treasure

  • There’s going to be some changes in your life: 2Corinthians 5:17 Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new.
  • You are going to receive the blessings of God: Psa 1:1 Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful.

Outro:

  • Have you found His treasure?
    • God intended His treasure to be found by you, if you are here this morning it’s yours. Will you accept it?
  • Maybe you are saved this morning?
    • What are you doing with His treasure. Are you at peace? Or are you in bondage to sin. Now is the time to redeem the treasure God has for you
