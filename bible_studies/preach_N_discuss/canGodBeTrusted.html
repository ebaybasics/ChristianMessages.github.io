The Foundation Series: 1 Cor 3:11 For other foundation can no man lay than that is laid, which is Jesus Christ. 

Can God be Trusted:

Joh 14:6  Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me. 

    Intro: Hard to trust anymore ("If your like me..." speak of my personal experiences)

    • Rough childhood (Started using drugs at a young age)
    • So-called friends that claimed to be on your side (People that had your back really had an interest in your wallet) Pro 29:5  A man that flattereth his neighbour spreadeth a net for his feet. 
    • Even the world breaks trust when you tried to do the right things (struggling with the most basic things like getting identification for a job)
    • God wants to intervene on your life (But God requires our trust in Him, so lets consider the trustworthiness of God?) Heb 11:6  But without faith it is impossible to please him: for he that cometh to God must believe that he is, and that he is a rewarder of them that diligently seek him. 

      Who God says He is:

    • He claims to be creator of allJoh 1:3  All things were made by him; and without him was not any thing made that was made. 
    • He claims to be able to give us a new life 2Co 5:17  Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new. 
    • He claims to be the provider of peace and restMat 11:28  Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest. 
      • What does God mean by "heavy laden" - carrying a burden that we werent designed to carry alone

      Evidences for Him:

    • Personal testimony
    • The cohesiveness of the Bible (There are over 66 books in the Bible, written over 2000 years apart but the character of God never changes.) 2Ti 3:16  All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness: 
      • Imagine a book series was written 500 years ago in America. Would the main character behave the same 200 years ago as he would in todays version?
    • Prophecies that have come true (Isaiah written 800 years before Jesus) Isa 7:14  Therefore the Lord himself shall give you a sign; Behold, a virgin shall conceive, and bear a son, and shall call his name Immanuel. 
    • God asks us to prove HimMal 3:10  Bring ye all the tithes into the storehouse, that there may be meat in mine house, and prove me now herewith, saith the LORD of hosts, if I will not open you the windows of heaven, and pour you out a blessing, that there shall not be room enough to receive it. 

      The reward of believing that He is who He says He is

    • Eternal life: (Its difficult to consider the importance) Joh 10:28  And I give unto them eternal life; and they shall never perish, neither shall any man pluck them out of my hand. 
    • The creator of the universe will be on your side: (If the One who spoke the universe into existence is on your team who could stand against you?)Rom 8:31  What shall we then say to these things? If God be for us, who can be against us? 
    • You will have reinforcements: (Our action plan) Gal 6:2  Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ. 
      • If you had someone on your team helping you get the things you need, would it make a difference?

      The consequence for not believing Him

    • Eternally separated from God: (The Bible calls this eternal separation "death") Rom 6:23  For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord. 
    • The truth will be your judge: (God put someone in your path with the truth)Joh 12:48  He that rejecteth me, and receiveth not my words, hath one that judgeth him: the word that I have spoken, the same shall judge him in the last day. 
      • What does Jesus mean when He says "the same shall judge him in the last day."
    • God designed it so none would have the excuse that they didnt know: (Creation itself is evidence, no systems from randomness)Rom_1:20  For the invisible things of him from the creation of the world are clearly seen, being understood by the things that are made, even his eternal power and Godhead; so that they are without excuse:
    • Weighing to risk\reward results(small risk, big reward)

      How to receive God's promises in your life

    • It takes believing He is who He says He is and knowing He is the only way: (Our text) Joh 14:6  Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me. 
    • Knowing it is us who separated ourselves from God through sin:(light cannot have fellowship with darkness)Rom 3:23  For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God; 
    • God loved us in the thick of our sin, and He built the bridge for our relationship to be repaired: Optional: Elaborate the Topic Rom 5:8  But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us. 
    • That we should turn from our way to His:(We must be tired of our way not working) Mat 4:17 “From that time Jesus began to preach, and to say, Repent: for the kingdom of heaven is at hand. “
    • And accept Him into our hearts as our Lord and Saviour:(It really is that simple, its us that overcomplicates it) Rom 10:9  That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved. 

      Closing:

    • Do you believe that God is who He says He is?
    • Are you ready to leave this old life behind and receive a new life?
    • Or will you reject God's intervention on your life
