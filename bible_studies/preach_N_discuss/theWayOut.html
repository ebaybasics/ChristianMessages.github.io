Title: The Way Out

Flagship Verse: Psa 62:5  My soul, wait thou only upon God; for my expectation is from him.  Psa 62:6  He only is my rock and my salvation: he is my defence; I shall not be moved.  Psa 62:7  In God is my salvation and my glory: the rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.  Psa 62:8  Trust in him at all times; ye people, pour out your heart before him: God is a refuge for us. Selah. 

Different Ways

  • Our Way Our way, being a man, leads to our destruction Rom 2:5  But after thy hardness and impenitent heart treasurest up unto thyself wrath against the day of wrath and revelation of the righteous judgment of God;
  • The World's Way The world tells us to pull ourselves up from the bootstrap - well intentioned but wrong Pro 14:12  There is a way which seemeth right unto a man, but the end thereof are the ways of death. 
  • The way of Jesus Jesus's way leads directly to fellowship with God Joh 14:6  Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me. 

What Does God's Way Offer?

  • Offers Hope The world lets you down, thats not the case when you put your hope in Him TEXT: My soul, wait thou only upon God; for my expectation is from him. 
  • Offers a Solid Defense When God is on your side, you are under His protection TEXT: He only is my rock and my salvation: he is my defence; I shall not be moved. 
  • Offers Salvation His way is the way out TEXT: Psa 62:7  In God is my salvation and my glory:
  • Offers Strength You lost motivation, His way will be your strength Psa 62:7  In God is my salvation and my glory: the rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God. 
  • Offers Loyalty The world may have forsaken you, but God will never leave or forsake you TEXT: Trust in him at all times;
  • Offers Refuge When the storms our raging, He is our shelter TEXT: God is a refuge for us.

What's Blocking The Path

  • Our Sin There is a price to be paid for what we've done, you touch a hot stove you get burned Rom 6:23  For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord.
  • Our Will It's our stubborn desire to keep doing it our own way thats killing us Jas 4:10  Humble yourselves in the sight of the Lord, and he shall lift you up. 
  • Our Unbelief Contrary to the worlds opinion, there is something that will keep you out of heaven Mat_12:31  Wherefore I say unto you, All manner of sin and blasphemy shall be forgiven unto men: but the blasphemy against the Holy Ghost shall not be forgiven unto men.

Can The Path Be Cleared? - The Good News

  • Our Sin Could Not Stop God's Love for Us (We wronged God and He still went to the cross for us) Rom 5:8  But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us. 
  • Surrender is Victory (When we turn from sin and turn to God we are made free) Rom_6:22  But now being made free from sin, and become servants to God, ye have your fruit unto holiness, and the end everlasting life.
  • Faith is required (The one thing God will not forgive, is our unbelief. He sent someone with the truth) Rom 10:9  That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved. 

Our Ministry: Hope

  • Welcome to Victory You are already half way through the first of 8 Sundays Isa 26:3  Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee: because he trusteth in thee. 
  • Fellow Soldiers If you choose to walk with us, we will carry your cross with you Gal 6:2  Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ. 
  • Overcome the world God will provide your needs, clothes, housing etc. But you must put Him first Mat 6:33  But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you.
