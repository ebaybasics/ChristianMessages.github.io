The Foundation:

1Co 3:11  For other foundation can no man lay than that is laid, which is Jesus Christ. 

  • Week 1: God: Who He Is and Why It Matters - Discovering who God is and why understanding His nature is essential to our lives.
  • Week 2: The Problem of Sin: Understanding Our Need for Salvation - Understanding the impact of sin on our lives and how it separates us from God.
  • Week 3: The Solution to Sin: The Good News of Jesus Christ - Learning about the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ and how it offers us salvation from sin.
  • Week 4: The Power of the Cross: Why Jesus' Death Matters - Understanding the significance of Jesus' death on the cross and how it makes salvation possible.
  • Week 5: The Resurrection: Proof of Jesus' Victory Over Death - Learning about the resurrection of Jesus and how it provides evidence of His power and love for us.
  • Week 6: The Holy Spirit: Our Helper and Guide - Discovering who the Holy Spirit is and how He works in our lives to guide and empower us.
  • Week 7: The Bible: God's Word for Us - Understanding the importance of the Bible and how it provides guidance and wisdom for our lives.
  • Week 8: Faith: Trusting in God's Promises - Exploring the meaning of faith and how we can trust in God's promises even when life is difficult.
  • Week 9: Prayer: Communication with God - Discovering the power of prayer and how it helps us connect with God.
  • Week 10: The Church: God's Plan for Community and Service - Learning about the purpose and importance of the church and how we can get involved in serving others.
  • Week 11: Living for God: Discipleship and Obedience - Understanding what it means to follow Jesus and how we can live a life of obedience to Him.
  • Week 12: The Great Commission: Sharing Your Faith - Discovering how we can share the good news of Jesus with others and make a difference in the world.
